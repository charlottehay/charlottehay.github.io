Best of the Best

Favorite Taft Moment

Wining our volleyball game on hotchkiss day 2022, on my serve with hundreds of people in the gym. Also, makinng gingerbread houses and going to finos with friends.

Most Overqualified Teacher

Okay considering I'm getting grdaded on this website this answer may be slightly biased, but it is 100% Mr. Rane. Everytime you talk to him you'll find out some absolutly crazy fact, his famous rock band, his reccord marathon times, his biking skills, his work on theorums, just casually working at Taft "for fun", the list is literally endless.

Best Taft Class

(Besides computer science obviously) Without a doubt, you should enroll in Engineering if you like STEM. The class is filled with absoliutly random projects, you have so much fun, never have homework, get to leave early, and come out having learned a lot about the field.

Worst Taft Class

Honors(AP) US History. Okay so I mayyyyy not be a history person. But that dosent take away from the fact that you have to annotate and KNOW 20 pages of material a night, and just basically discuss it all class. Nevermind the 6 page research paper. I loved my teacher, but this class was NOT for me.

Taft Tips

1: Do homework the night its assigned. 2: Try and be a friend to everyone, even if they might not be your favorite person. 3: Make friends with Mr. Burnett and Cheryl in the package room. 4: Go to bed BEFORE midnight.
